Use of Resistance Bands

This week we will utilising the use of elastic bands to help increase hip strength and mobilisation so please ensure that you bring a range of bands to training. If you don't have any bands yet I have some spares that you can utilise but please go online tonight and purchase a black, red and purple band, these normally cost between £1.50 - £3 each.

To help you prepare for this activity please refer to the attached link and video for some of the exercises we will be using. Please have a look and try to understand how the band focuses on the key areas I have highlighted earlier - there will be a test so be prepared!!
